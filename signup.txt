SIGNUP

Endpoint
--------
/api/singup/v1

Verb
----
POST

Parameters
----------
* email (required)
* name
* phone
* gender
* first_name
* last_name
* middle_name
* street
* city
* country_code
* dob (date of birth)
* url
* meta1
* meta2
* meta3
* data (site specific)
* utm_xxxx variables

Control Parameters
------------------
* return_url (where to go after successful signup)
* failure_url (where to go when signup failed)
* login (if set to any value, then will also login user after successful signup)
